Biography

Samuel Vincent was born in Vancouver, Canada on October 5, 1971. Notable for his voice acting work, he contributed to the animated television shows Littlest Pet Shop and Ed, Edd n Eddy, as well as to the anime productions Video Girl Ai and Tokyo Underground. He and fellow voiceover artist Matt Hill often performed together; their collaborations include Gundam SEED and Monster Buster Club. He began his voice acting career in the early 1990s.
